# Ilova Professional Qilish Requirements

## Introduction

Quyosh24 ilovasini to'liq professional darajaga olib chiqish - barcha funksiyalarni tekshirish, UI/UX yaxshilash, performance optimizatsiya va release tayyorlash.

## Glossary

- **App**: Quyosh24 ilovasi
- **UI/UX**: User Interface va User Experience
- **Performance**: Ilova tezligi va samaradorligi
- **Release**: Google Play uchun tayyor versiya

## Requirements

### Requirement 1: Barcha Ekranlar Ishlashi

**User Story:** Foydalanuvchi sifatida, men barcha ekranlarning to'g'ri ishlashini xohlayman.

#### Acceptance Criteria

1. THE App SHALL barcha ekranlarni xatosiz ko'rsatadi
2. THE App SHALL barcha navigation'lar to'g'ri ishlaydi
3. THE App SHALL barcha ma'lumotlar to'g'ri yuklanadi
4. THE App SHALL offline rejimda ham ishlaydi

### Requirement 2: UI/UX Professional

**User Story:** Foydalanuvchi sifatida, men chiroyli va professional interfeys'ni xohlayman.

#### Acceptance Criteria

1. THE App SHALL zamonaviy va chiroyli dizaynga ega bo'ladi
2. THE App SHALL barcha ranglar uyg'unlashadi
3. THE App SHALL animatsiyalar silliq bo'ladi
4. THE App SHALL loading states professional ko'rinadi

### Requirement 3: Performance Optimizatsiya

**User Story:** Foydalanuvchi sifatida, men ilovaning tez ishlashini xohlayman.

#### Acceptance Criteria

1. THE App SHALL 2 soniyadan kamroq vaqtda ishga tushadi
2. THE App SHALL rasmlar optimizatsiya qilingan bo'ladi
3. THE App SHALL memory efficient bo'ladi
4. THE App SHALL smooth scrolling'ga ega bo'ladi

### Requirement 4: Release Tayyorlash

**User Story:** Dasturchi sifatida, men Google Play uchun tayyor APK yaratishni xohlayman.

#### Acceptance Criteria

1. THE App SHALL ProGuard/R8 obfuscation bilan build qilinadi
2. THE App SHALL signed APK bo'ladi
3. THE App SHALL app bundle (AAB) formatida bo'ladi
4. THE App SHALL barcha permissions to'g'ri sozlangan bo'ladi
